# Data Quality Checklist

## Before collection

- Confirm participant/session ID is correct.
- Confirm visit date and study timepoint.
- Confirm required forms and measures.
- Confirm device readiness and calibration, if applicable.
- Confirm protocol version.

## During collection

- Document deviations immediately.
- Flag missing or questionable values.
- Record device issues or interruptions.
- Follow naming conventions.

## After collection

- Verify file completeness.
- Confirm REDCap/source documentation match.
- Log corrections according to SOP.
- Store files in the approved secure location.
- Prepare notes for investigator or team review.
