# Project 5: Clinical Research Operations Toolkit

## Purpose

Organize public-facing templates and examples that demonstrate clinical research coordination, documentation, and protocol-driven study operations.

## NASA / contractor relevance

NASA human research and contractor roles often require clear documentation, participant coordination, study tracking, data quality, compliance awareness, and communication across technical and clinical teams.

## Toolkit contents

- Visit tracker template
- Data quality checklist
- SOP outline
- CRF planning template
- Regulatory binder index
- Stakeholder meeting notes template

## Privacy note

Use generic templates only. Do not upload real study documents, controlled materials, participant identifiers, PHI, CUI, or employer-owned documents.
