# Morgan Robinson | NASA / Space Medicine Portfolio

This portfolio organizes projects at the intersection of human performance research, operational medicine, neurotechnology, wearable data, rehabilitation systems, NASA open science, and Earth observation.

## Portfolio focus

- Human performance and operational medicine
- Space medicine and aerospace-adjacent biomedical research
- Wearable biometric data and recovery monitoring
- Neurotechnology, dry EEG/qEEG, and PrTMS-support workflows
- Rehabilitation technology, exoskeleton mobility, and prosthetic control
- NASA Earth observation, environmental exposure, and decision-support applications
- Reproducible research, FAIR data practices, and open science

## Repository structure

| Folder | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `01_earth_observation_human_performance` | Connects Earth observation exposure data to human performance and operational health questions. |
| `02_wearable_recovery_dashboard` | Organizes wearable sleep/recovery data concepts and dashboard planning. |
| `03_remote_sensing_worldview_case_study` | Stores NASA Worldview imagery notes, screenshots, and case study write-ups. |
| `04_space_medicine_literature_matrix` | Tracks literature on space medicine, human adaptation, neurocognition, sleep, inflammation, and operational performance. |
| `05_clinical_research_operations_toolkit` | Stores templates for SOPs, CRFs, visit tracking, regulatory binders, and protocol-driven research operations. |
| `data` | Placeholder for raw and processed datasets. Do not upload protected or identifiable data. |
| `docs` | Portfolio documents, project summaries, and public-facing PDFs. |
| `assets` | Public screenshots, figures, diagrams, and images. |

## Current training and technical foundation

- NASA ARSET Online Self-Paced Certificate
- NIH Introduction to the Principles and Practice of Clinical Research
- Good Clinical Practice, Human Subjects Protection, HIPAA/Privacy Act, CUI, DoD Cyber Awareness
- REDCap, Excel, Python, C++, EthoVision, Oura Ring exports, dry EEG/qEEG workflows

## Data privacy note

This portfolio is designed for public-facing, de-identified, synthetic, or open-source data only. Do not upload participant identifiers, protected health information, CUI, controlled records, raw clinical data, or any non-public federal/DoD materials.
