# Project brief: Earth Observation + Human Performance

## Problem

Operational, clinical, and remote populations may experience environmental stressors that affect sleep, recovery, mobility, neurocognition, pain, and mission readiness.

## Users

- Human performance researchers
- Operational medicine teams
- Rehabilitation researchers
- Public health or environmental health decision-makers
- NASA / aerospace medicine stakeholders

## Application concept

Use Earth observation data as an exposure layer that can be paired with wearable, clinical, or performance outcomes to support risk screening, research design, and decision support.

## Example use case

A team wants to understand whether heat and wildfire smoke exposure are associated with changes in sleep recovery, fatigue, or mobility outcomes in an operational population.

## Success criteria

- Uses public or synthetic data only
- Clearly explains the environmental exposure variable
- Produces a simple visualization
- Connects the result to a human performance decision
- Documents limitations
